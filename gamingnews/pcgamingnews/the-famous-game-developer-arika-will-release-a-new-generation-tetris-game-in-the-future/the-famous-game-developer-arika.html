The famous game developer Arika will release a new generation Tetris game in the future.

Monday, Sept. 30, 20:41h
By TN Agency

According to the latest information, the renowned game developer Arika is developing and preparing to launch a completely new Tetris game.

Tetris, or the block-stacking game, is one of the classic titles for many gamers from the 80s and 90s generation. After more than 30 years of development and evolution, Tetris can be considered a famous game IP and one of the most engaging game genres today.

On the morning of September 27, the game developer Arika, one of the well-known names in the Japanese gaming industry, announced that they are developing a completely new Tetris game with gameplay under the license of "The Tetris Company."

If you didn't know, Arika is a game developer that has released a number of blockbuster titles, the most famous being Street Fighter, Tekken, and Endless Ocean. This company also has significant experience in developing Tetris games in the past, with the standout title being the highly popular Tetris 99 on Nintendo Switch.

According to the information shared by Arika, this new Tetris game will feature completely new gameplay. It will also help players improve their Tetris skills through continuous play. While not much information has been revealed yet, the game is expected to launch in early 2025. If you are a hardcore fan of Tetris, be sure to follow 2Game for the latest updates.
