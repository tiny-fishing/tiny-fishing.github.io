Dunkbrush: A Unique Basketball Skill Game

Dunkbrush is an engaging basketball skill game developed by Tiny Fishing. In this game, players will experience the feeling of becoming an artist with the task of drawing lines for the ball to go into the basket. Let's explore the interesting and captivating aspects of Dunkbrush in this article.

Developer: Tiny Fishing

Tiny Fishing is an independent game development company based in the United States. Founded in 2018, the company has quickly become one of the favorite names in the mobile gaming industry. Particularly, Tiny Fishing's products always carry the American spirit with beautiful graphics and compelling storylines.

And Dunkbrush is one of the proud releases by Tiny Fishing. With a combination of sports and art genres, Dunkbrush has quickly attracted the attention of the gaming community.

Description of Dunkbrush

Dunkbrush is a basketball genre game with simple yet refined graphics. You don't need to be a "pro" gamer or have high skills; just mastering the drawing and analyzing the trajectory of the ball will allow you to participate in this game.

In Dunkbrush, players will be challenged with different levels, from easy to hard. Each level has a different difficulty level, requiring players to be agile and have good drawing skills to complete the tasks.

How to play Dunkbrush

To start the game, simply click and drag the mouse to draw a straight line. The ball will move along the line you draw, and only when the ball goes into the basket can you proceed to the next level. This may be a simple gameplay, but not everyone can complete it quickly.

In addition to drawing straight lines, players can use tricks to help the ball go into the basket more easily. For example, drawing spirals, loops, or curves to make the ball move along different trajectories.

Principles of the game

To complete a level, players must make the ball go into the basket within the allowed number of drawing times. If you draw too many times and the ball falls to the ground, you will have to start over. Therefore, skill and agility are crucial in this game.

Moreover, speed and accuracy are also two evaluated factors in Dunkbrush. The faster and more accurate you are, the higher your score will be. However, balancing these two factors is also necessary to efficiently complete a level.

Collect coins to unlock new paint colors

A special feature of Dunkbrush is the ability to collect coins to unlock new paint colors. When completing a level, players will receive an amount of money corresponding to the achieved score. This money can be used to purchase new paint colors to change the color of the lines.

However, not all colors can be unlocked immediately. Players must complete certain levels and have enough money to unlock that color. This creates a challenging factor and makes the game more interesting.

Drawing lines to make the ball go into the basket

As mentioned earlier, drawing lines is the main gameplay of Dunkbrush. However, not everyone can draw a perfect line from the first time. The important thing is to grasp the trajectory of the ball and draw a correct line for it to go into the basket.

If you draw too high or too low, the ball will not go into the basket. This requires players to have observation skills and considerations to draw the line appropriately. Additionally, drawing spirals, loops, or curves will be a new challenge and require high concentration.

Analyzing the trajectory of the ball

Analyzing the trajectory of the ball is an important factor in Dunkbrush. A ball will never move along a perfect straight line, so players need to grasp the trajectory of the ball to draw an appropriate line.

The trajectory of the ball will depend on the initial force and direction of it. This can vary depending on the starting position of the ball and obstacles in the way. Therefore, to succeed in Dunkbrush, players need to have the ability to analyze and predict the trajectory of the ball accurately.
