Gameplay and Controls

In "Basket and Ball," you play as a basketball player trying to score more points than your opponent. Similar to real basketball gameplay, you'll control your character's movement on the court and make shots to score points. However, in this game, you'll not only be shooting the ball into the hoop but also navigating through obstacles and opposing players to reach your goal.

The controls in "Basket and Ball" are simple and easy to understand. Player one uses WASD to move and press F to shoot, while player two uses the arrow keys to move and the Enter key to shoot.

However, despite its simplicity, these controls are challenging and require focus and skill from the player. You need quick and accurate movements to overcome obstacles and score points, making the game more interesting and engaging.
